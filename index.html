Instructions:

  • Click on any card to reveal it
  • click two cards and check if they match each other.
  • If you found two identical cards they will stay revealed.
  • If you click 2 cards and they don't match they will hide again.
  • Try to remember the location of each card so later when you find it you can match it with the previous cards.
  • To win you must find the matches for all the cards.
  • The faster you are able to match all cards the more score you will get.
